About

Hello! My name is Andria Trigeorgi and I am a second year PhD student working under the supervision of Dr. Chryssis Georgiou in the Computer Science Department at the University of Cyprus. I hold an MSc (2016-2018) and a BSc (2012-2016) in Computer Science both from the same university. My research focuses on Distributed Computing. I am particularly interested in Machine Learning.
